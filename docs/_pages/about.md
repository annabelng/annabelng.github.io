Annabel Ng is a recent graduate from Westview High, and an incoming freshman at UC Berkeley studying electrical engineering and computer science. Having worked on suicide prevention and mental health campaigns in her school community as a Peer Counselor, Annabel is inspired to listen and amplify the voices of others. Her passion for outreach also applies to STEM education advocacy, where she developed and facilitated online workshops and speaker panels to encourage more girls to pursue STEM
as part of her school’s Society of Women Engineers chapter. When Annabel isn’t working on machine learning projects related to the healthcare space, she enjoys staying active by playing field hockey and running.
